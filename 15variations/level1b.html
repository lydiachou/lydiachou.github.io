Begin
with
ideas.                     Be first
                              or last.
     Embrace
     chance.                           Believe complex
                                            ideas can produce
                                            simple things.
          Celebrate
          coincidence.
                                                     Trust
                                                     the
                                                     process.
               Ad–lib and
               make things
               up.                                       Allow concepts
                                                           to determine
                                                           form.
          Eliminate
          superfluous
          elements.                            Reduce material
                                                     and production
                                                     to their essence.
     Subvert
     expectation.
                                            Sustain the
                                            integrity of
                                            an idea.
Make something
difficult look
easy.                             Propose
                                     honesty as
                                     a solution.


MINI–MANIFESTO
Daniel Eatock
